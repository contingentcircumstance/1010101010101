milo matukaitis is an artist living/working in Chicago Il and attending the School of the Art Institute of Chicago (bfa prospective '24).

Milo's practice is rooted in a continual exploration of the ways humans practice production, reproduction, and convolution of language and meaning making. Through both the practice of creating digital poetics, as well as through their exploration of physical material, their work investigates what processes and contexts go into the creation of an "artifact."
